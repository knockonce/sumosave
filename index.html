About SUMOSAVE

Sumosave is a large neighborhood supermarket which offers a relevant range of food & grocery and home needs products to its customers. Our stores carry daily use products in categories such as staples, packaged grocery, personal care, home care, beverages, dairy, fruits & vegetable, kitchen, and home needs.

We provide best value possible for our customers, so they can get maximum saving on their shopping when shopping at Sumosave supermarkets.

Why should you choose Sumosave?

Savings

we give you savings on your everyday grocery products which matter to you. All our savings are transparent and come on the bill. Our aim is to maximise savings on products which are important to you. We try and save everwhere so that we can pass on the savings to you. If you feel that our prices are high on any product, tell us and we will aim to lower our prices.

Close to you

our stores are in your neighborhood, close to where you live. There is no need to travel out to get the best savings in the city! We offer free parking and all conveniences.

Quality

our focus on quality is unwavering. We understand that we must offer you products of the highest quality while maximizing your savings. The Sumosave team will work hard to source the best quality products for you.

We encourage suppliers to collaborate with us and be a part of a commercially rewarding relationship while also building a long line of loyal customers.
